Fried Chicken Recipe

Try the Assumpta fried chicken recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of chicken
  • 250 ml of buttermilk
  • 250 grams all- purpose flour
  • 1 litre of cooking oil
  • salt
  • paprika
  • black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into pieces
  2. Place the chicken in a bowl,add salt and buttermilk
  3. Marinate for atleast 4hours for best results
  4. In a separate bowl,whisk together the flour,salt,paprika and black pepper
  5. Remove chicken pieces from the buttermilk and coat them in the seasoned flour mixture
  6. Heat oil in a large pot to medium heat until it sizzles and then carefully add the coated chicken pieces
  7. Fry until golden brown and cooked through,then remove to a cooking rack to drain excess oil before serving
  8. Serve while hot
